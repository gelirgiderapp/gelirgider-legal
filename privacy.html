Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Gelir Gider ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects the personal information you ("you", "user") provide while using our mobile application available on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information that you voluntarily provide while using the app:

  • Full Name
  • Age
  • Expense details (amount, category, description)
  • Income details (amount, source, description)
  • General financial notes

No sensitive data (such as biometric, health, or banking credentials) is collected.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal data is used solely for the core functionality of the app, including:

  • Saving and displaying your income and expense history
  • Helping you track your financial habits
  • Generating basic statistical overviews

We do not use your data for advertising, tracking, or profiling.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device unless otherwise stated. We take reasonable precautions to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Third-Party Access

We do not share your personal information with any third parties. No analytics or advertising tools are integrated into the app.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • View the data you entered
  • Edit or delete your information
  • Permanently remove your data by uninstalling the app

6. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. All changes will be posted here with an updated date.
